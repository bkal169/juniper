# Distribution Matrix — Lumena OS

## Overview

Every piece of primary content gets repurposed across platforms using defined rules. This eliminates guesswork and ensures maximum reach from minimum production effort.

## Platform Specifications

### YouTube
- **Long-form:** 8–20 min, 1920x1080, MP4
- **Shorts:** 30–60s, 1080x1920 vertical
- **Thumbnail:** 1280x720, bold text, high contrast face
- **Description:** First 2 lines = hook + CTA, then timestamps, links, hashtags
- **Tags:** 8–15 relevant tags, mix of broad and specific
- **Post time:** Mon/Wed/Fri 10:00 AM ET

### Instagram
- **Reels:** 30–60s, 1080x1920, native captions, trending audio optional
- **Carousel:** 1080x1080 or 1080x1350, 5–10 slides, CTA on last slide
- **Stories:** 1080x1920, polls/questions for engagement
- **Caption:** Hook in first line, value in body, CTA + hashtags at end
- **Hashtags:** 15–20, mix of niche (10K–100K) and broad (100K–1M)
- **Post time:** Mon–Sat 11:00 AM or 7:00 PM ET

### TikTok
- **Video:** 30–90s, 1080x1920, native captions required
- **Caption:** Short hook, 3–5 hashtags
- **Sound:** Trending audio when relevant, original audio for talking head
- **Post time:** Tue/Thu/Sat 12:00 PM ET

### LinkedIn
- **Text post:** Hook line → 3–5 short paragraphs → question for engagement
- **Article:** Long-form adaptation of blog content
- **Image post:** Quote graphic or data visualization
- **Carousel (PDF):** Upload as document for swipeable carousel
- **Post time:** Tue/Wed/Thu 8:00 AM ET

### X (Twitter)
- **Thread:** 5–8 tweets, first tweet = hook, last = CTA + link
- **Single tweet:** Hot take, stat, or question for engagement
- **Image:** 1600x900 for link previews, 1080x1080 for standalone
- **Post time:** Mon–Fri 9:00 AM or 5:00 PM ET

### Email (ConvertKit/Substack)
- **Frequency:** Weekly (Thursday)
- **Format:** 1 big idea + 2–3 bullets + 1 CTA
- **Subject line:** Under 50 chars, curiosity-driven or benefit-driven
- **Preview text:** Complement (not repeat) the subject line
- **Send time:** Thursday 9:00 AM ET

## Repurpose Workflow

```
Primary Content Published
    │
    ├── Day 0: Extract 2–3 short clips (video) or key quotes (written)
    │          Create platform-native versions (captions, aspect ratios)
    │
    ├── Day 1: Post clips/derivatives to social platforms
    │          Create LinkedIn adaptation
    │          Schedule X thread
    │
    ├── Day 2–3: Write blog adaptation (if primary was video/podcast)
    │            Create carousel/graphics for Instagram
    │
    ├── Day 5–7: Include in weekly newsletter
    │
    └── Day 30: Review performance → flag top performers for re-promotion
```

---

## Cross-Promotion Rules

1. **Always link back** to the primary piece from derivatives
2. **Never post identical copy** across platforms — adapt voice and format
3. **Stagger posting** — don't flood all platforms in the same hour
4. **Use UTM parameters** on all links for attribution tracking
5. **Tag/mention** relevant people, brands, or topics on each platform
6. **Engage for 15 min** after posting on any platform (reply to comments)

---

## UTM Parameter Convention

```
?utm_source=[platform]&utm_medium=[format]&utm_campaign=[content-slug]

Examples:
?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=reel&utm_campaign=first-time-buyer-mistakes
?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=q3-market-update
?utm_source=email&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=weekly-2026-04-03
```
